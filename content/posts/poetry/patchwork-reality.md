title: The Seamstress' Patchwork Reality
date: 2023-03-03
---

Tethered, ringing,
Broken bones,
Each had sound
Their hollow tone.

Lead's the servant,
Of these observant,
Eyes that fled,
The ghastly seams.

And found themselves
Lost trapped in dreams,
In the seamstress
And her scenery.
